 STEP 4 — Interpretation of the Round 9 gap fixes
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

 FIX 1 — Signal stack wired through. The "Signal-source frequency" panels
   above confirm that the adapter is exercising every branch of the
   production stack:
     * pre-ML :  rule-based primary fires 360 times, pattern contributes 15
                 additional votes, ML is inactive (_ml_active = 0).
     * post-ML:  ML classifier fires 158 times (_ml_active = 1), pattern
                 contributes 20 votes, the rule-based fallback is not used.
   ORB never contributes because its get_signal() is time-gated to live
   ET 10:05-15:00 intraday windows and is structurally unusable on daily
   OHLCV. The adapter still routes the empty vote through the aggregator
   to match the production path exactly.

 FIX 2 — Walk-forward Sharpe is now NaN-safe. Every one of the 23 folds
   reports NaN because 63-day OOS windows at this position size produce
   fewer than 5 daily returns per fold (most have 0-2 trades). In Round 8
   these folds silently reported 0.0 Sharpe, which inflated the positive-
   fold count. The fix:
     1. Sharpe = NaN when fewer than 5 daily returns or std < 1e-8.
     2. Mean / median Sharpe exclude NaN folds from aggregation.
     3. insufficient_data_folds (23/23 here) is reported alongside
        positive_folds and negative_folds.
   Compounded return across the folds is 0.0% — no fold actually produced
   a closed trade stream deep enough for a valid Sharpe.

 FIX 3 — Baseline ML classifier trained, audited and loaded. Leakage
   audit cleared with leakage_check.ok = True and zero leaky features.
   Training accuracy 0.7576 is consistent with healthy in-sample fit;
   test accuracy 0.4730 is below 50% on the held-out 2022 window,
   reflecting regime drift on the down-heavy 2022 market vs. the 2018-
   2021 training distribution. Baseline signed-score stats (mean 0.2278,
   std 0.2683) are pickled alongside the model so the adapter's GAP-8B
   drift guard can reject scores more than 4 sigma from calibration.
