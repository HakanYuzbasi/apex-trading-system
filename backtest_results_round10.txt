 Round 10 addresses three gaps from Round 9:

   PROBLEM 1 — Threshold too aggressive (13 trades/year in Round 9).
               Calibrated the entry gate to the P40 of the 2023
               |primary_signal| distribution produced by the ML model
               (750 non-zero samples, P40 = 0.189676), and surfaced it
               as ApexConfig.ML_CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD (default 0.19,
               env-overridable via APEX_ML_CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD).
               See backtesting/real_signal_adapter.py:201-238 and
               config.py:2394-2407.

   PROBLEM 2 — One model trained, saved to all three regime paths.
               Rewrote scripts/train_baseline_models.py to classify
               each training bar by ADX(14) and ATR(14)/price:
                 * TRENDING  — ADX > 25
                 * MEAN_REV  — ADX < 20 AND ATR/price < 0.015
                 * VOLATILE  — ATR/price >= 0.015
               Each regime is trained on its own subset (or falls back
               to the full-dataset model when <200 rows). The adapter
               (_select_regime_model + _ml_primary) now routes every
               inference to the regime-appropriate .pkl.

   PROBLEM 3 — Walk-forward Sharpe NaN on all 23 folds (63-bar OOS too
               short). Raised WF_OOS_BARS default from 63 to 126
               (env-overridable) and expanded the symbol universe to
               7 tickers (AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, SPY, QQQ, NVDA, AMZN).
